Our Story

Where wellness meets ritual

AZAI was born from the belief that movement is medicine — that showing up for your body is the most powerful commitment you can make to yourself. Nestled in Jal El Dib, we built more than a studio: we built an experience.

BRUME Coffee Bar grew naturally from that vision. Because real wellness doesn't stop when the class ends. It lingers over a slow pour, a pastry, and a moment to breathe. Together, AZAI and BRUME create a sanctuary where every visit feels like coming home.

"You walk in carrying the day. You walk out lighter."
